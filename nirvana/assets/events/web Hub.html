Web-Hub

Description

Get ready to put your website development skills to the test in our exciting competition! Compete against other talented developers to create the best website within a given time limit. Show off your creativity, technical skills, and attention to detail for a chance to win great prizes and recognition. Whether you're a student, a professional, or just love to code, this event is a must-attend for anyone interested in website development.

Team: 1

Entry fees: Rs.50/-

Rules and regulations

  1. Entry Fee is 50rs per team.
  2. Participants need to design a 5+ pages website.
  3. Website has to be created before May 5th 2023.
  4. Participants have to design a web-site on any one of the specified topics and present it to the judges during the event.
  5. First page must be your Homepage (Containing LOGO/picture depicting the topic and the website name).
  6. The winners will be selected by the judges for best designed web page/site and their decision will be final.
  7. Registration Fees will not be refunded.
  8. ID card for all college Students is mandatory.
