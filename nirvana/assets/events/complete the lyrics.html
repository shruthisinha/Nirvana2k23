Finish THE LYRICS

Description

Get ready to belt out your favorite tunes at our Complete the Lyrics event! Sing your heart out and compete against other music lovers to see who can finish the lyrics to the most songs. With great music, prizes, and a lively atmosphere, it's sure to be a night to remember!

Team: 2

Entry fees: Rs.100/- per team

1st prize:1000/-
2nd prize:500/

Rules and regulations

  1. Entry Fee is 100rs per team.
  2. Team of 2 members.
  3. Languages:Kannada, English, Hindi
  4. A bowl contain the chits of above language, depending on the chit selected songs will be played.
  5. Should guess the lyrics within 10 sec.
  6. Team guessing most of the correct lyrics will be considered the winner.
  7. Registration Fees will not be refunded.
  8. ID card for all colleges is mandatory.
